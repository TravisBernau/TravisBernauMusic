ABOUT

Music that supports the player’s journey

I’m Travis Bernau, a composer and game-audio creative specializing in music for video games, film, and interactive media. My work draws on orchestral writing, atmosphere, thematic storytelling, and collaborative development.

I’m especially interested in fantasy RPGs, science-fiction, horror, medieval settings, and emotionally rich narrative experiences.
